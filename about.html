About PikiShield

We exist to protect
those who keep Kenya moving.

Boda boda riders power Kenya's last-mile economy. We built PikiShield to make sure they never ride unprotected.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible mutual aid cover to every boda boda rider in Kenya — so they can ride with confidence, knowing their families are protected.

🌍

Our Vision

A Kenya where no rider's family faces financial ruin from a roadside arrest, injury, or death. Where protection is as accessible as a daily fare.

💚

Our Promise

Fast claims. Transparent processes. Real money in your M-Pesa when you need it most. We are in the business of paying out — not finding reasons not to.

Our Story

Born from a real problem on Kenya's roads

Every day, thousands of boda boda riders are arrested at roadblocks, injured in accidents, or lose family members — with no financial safety net. Banks won't lend to them. Traditional cover is too expensive and too complicated.

PikiShield was built to change that. We created a simple, mobile-first mutual aid platform that works for the way riders actually live — daily contributions, M-Pesa claims, and a community that has each other's back.

We started in Nairobi and we're growing across all 47 counties. Because every rider deserves to ride with confidence.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make at PikiShield.

🤝

Community First

We exist for riders, not shareholders. Every feature, every decision, every hire is made with the rider community in mind.

⚡

Speed Matters

When a rider is in trouble, every hour counts. We obsess over fast claims processing because delays cost lives and livelihoods.

🔍

Radical Transparency

No hidden clauses. No confusing fine print. We tell riders exactly what they're covered for — in plain Swahili and English.

📱

Mobile First

Riders live on their phones. So do we. Everything — registration, payments, claims — works seamlessly on any smartphone.

🌱

Sustainable Impact

We build for the long term. Our model is designed to grow with Kenya's boda boda sector for generations to come.

🛡️

Trust Above All

Riders trust us with their family's financial security. We honour that trust by always paying legitimate claims — no questions asked.
